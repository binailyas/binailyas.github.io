About Kahf

Kahf represents a new standard in men’s care, a standard where performance is not just about results, but also about purpose and intention. It is designed to go beyond basic grooming, offering a more meaningful approach to self-care that aligns with a man’s values and daily life.

Built for those who are constantly striving to grow, Kahf supports individuals who seek progress in every aspect of their journey. Whether it’s exploring new opportunities, challenging themselves, or simply becoming a better version of who they were yesterday, Kahf is there as a companion.

Kahf is a men’s grooming brand from Indonesia, developed by Paragon Technology and Innovation, the company behind Wardah, Make Over, and Emina. Since its launch in 2020, Kahf has focused on addressing the growing need for men’s personal care in urban lifestyles, where awareness of self-care and appearance continues to increase alongside modern daily demands.

The brand offers a range of products including facial wash, skincare, sunscreen, fragrance, and body care, designed to be simple, effective, and easy to use. Built on the principles of halal, practical, and natural, Kahf ensures that every product aligns with the values and daily needs of modern men, while maintaining a balance between functionality, comfort, and reliability in everyday routines.
